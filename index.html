From file to living world in 4 steps

No tutorials needed. No steep learning curves. Just pure creative joy.

1

Import

Drag in a PNG, JPG, or GLB file. Thumbnails generate automatically. Your assets are cataloged and ready to place.

2

Place

Drop your asset into a 3D terrarium. Camera, ground, and lighting are already set up and looking beautiful.

3

Animate

Assign a behavior like "Wander" or "Idle." Watch subtle wiggle and movement bring personality to your creation.

4

Enjoy

Save your world and come back anytime. It loads exactly as you left it, ready to grow and evolve.

Built on strong principles

These aren't just features. They're commitments to how creative tools should work.

Not a Game Engine

No complexity tax. No overwhelming feature lists. Digital Terrarium does one thing beautifully: it lets your creative work exist in space, with just enough motion to feel alive.

Local First, Cloud Optional

Inspired by Obsidian's file-ownership model, your creations live on your machine in simple, readable formats. The cloud is an option for sharing, never a requirement for creating.

Note: Cloud features are not available yet.

Charming Imperfection

A cozy, slightly janky aesthetic by choice. This isn't about photorealism or AAA polish. It's about warmth, personality, and the joy of seeing something you made come to life.

Artist-First Language

We say "wiggle" not "oscillation." Behaviors, not state graphs. Terrarium, not scene. The language shapes the experience, and we choose words that feel creative, not technical.
